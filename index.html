About me

I’m a full-time traveler, student and content creator driven by a love for discovery and connection. For me, travel isn’t just about visiting new places – it's about pushing personal boundaries, establishing worldwide friendships and finding beauty in the unexpected.

At 17, I moved from Norway to Costa Rica as the first step toward realizing my dream of traveling the world. Since then, I’ve studied abroad in Spain and Vietnam, volunteered on various continents and I’ve explored countless countries, embracing the beauty of diverse landscapes, cultures and people. Travel has shaped every part of my life, blending into both my personal and academic experiences. Now, I share my adventures as a content creator, focusing on budget travel and volunteering abroad, which promote sustainable, meaningful travel and giving back while exploring new destinations.

Travel lets me embrace a spontaneous, carefree lifestyle where every day brings something new. I thrive on adventure, often packing my bags on a whim to discover hidden gems. Whether it’s hitchhiking my way through Nicaragua, a road trip with strangers in the Italian Alps or teaching children at a Buddhist temple in Sri Lanka, I’m up for anything. Let’s travel the world together!
